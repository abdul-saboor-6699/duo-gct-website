About Program

Program Overview

Electrical Engineering is one of the largest and broadest engineering disciplines that deals with the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. The scope and demand for Electrical technology is very high in Pakistan. There are more job opportunities for electrical engineers than in any other field of engineering.

Pakistan is still facing energy crises, and the need for more powerhouses and renewable energy sources is being felt, which will definitely result in more demand for electrical engineers.
