Program Overview

Pakistan's booming construction sector demands skilled civil technicians. With CPEC infrastructure projects, housing schemes, dams, and road networks under development, the opportunities for Civil Technology graduates are immense. Our program prepares students for careers in surveying, structural design, construction supervision, and quantity estimation.

This 3-year DAE diploma covers surveying techniques, structural analysis, construction materials, AutoCAD, and project management. Students gain practical experience through site visits, laboratory testing, and real-world projects.

Program Highlights

  • Modern Surveying Equipment
  • AutoCAD & Civil 3D Training
  • Material Testing Laboratory
