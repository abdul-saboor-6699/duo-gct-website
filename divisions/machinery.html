Program Overview

The Mechanical Technology program at GCT Bhakkar prepares students for Pakistan's growing manufacturing and industrial sector. With the rise of CPEC projects, automotive industry, and textile machinery, the demand for skilled mechanical technicians has never been higher. Our graduates work in factories, power plants, construction sites, and engineering firms across the country.

This 3-year diploma covers machine design, manufacturing processes, CAD/CAM, welding, CNC programming, and industrial maintenance. Students gain hands-on experience in our fully equipped workshops with lathes, milling machines, and modern fabrication tools.
