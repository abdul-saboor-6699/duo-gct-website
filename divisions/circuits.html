Program Overview

The Electronics Technology program prepares students for Pakistan's rapidly growing tech industry. From mobile phone assembly plants to telecommunications, from medical equipment manufacturing to defense electronics, the demand for skilled electronics technicians continues to surge. Our graduates work with leading companies in embedded systems, IoT, and communication technologies.

This 3-year DAE diploma covers circuit design, microcontrollers, digital electronics, communication systems, and PCB fabrication. Students gain hands-on experience with oscilloscopes, function generators, and modern testing equipment.
